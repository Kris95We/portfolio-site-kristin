About the App

YUMMY is a student portfolio web app design that suggests recipes based on the customers’ calorie needs and budget. The app and its design aim to attract confident users who value conscious nutrition within every price range.

Hypothesis

Users are looking for a web app which is easy to handle, to search for meals and recipes. Users further wish to get recipe suggestions within different price ranges, depending for what range the user filters. Users need to adjust the measurement of ingredients by selecting the portion sizes. Users of the web app can search for recipes with specific ingredients by entering them into the search bar.

Proposal

Yummy is a meal planning app that focuses on simplifying daily cooking and what is needed to do so. Users have filters like budget and calorie to get specific recipe suggestions and a file to save and view their favorite recipes. Creating an account is optional and not needed for most of the apps’ functionalities, to make it easier for everyone to get started.
