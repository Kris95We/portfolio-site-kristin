Student Portfolio Project "Barking Buddies"

Barking Buddies

Find a furry friend for your dog!

Problem Statement

Not all dogs get along well. Dog’s have different personalities, which don’t match, and owners have already notices that. But there is nothing better for a dog than playing with other dogs and their pawrents can only partially compensate this. So why don’t we use an app that matches dog’s personalities nearby?

Challenges

  • Meeting the same dogs on every walk.
  • Many dogs have problematic characteristics and hence can’t or don’t want to play.
  • Puppies need good first encounters to develop a healthy relationship to other dog’s and humans.
  • Leash requirement in many countries. Dog’s can’t fully enjoy playing.
  • Burden of networking on other platforms.

Solution

Introducing Barking Buddies, the solution to find new friends for your dog just a paw swipe away. Available in iOS and Android, Barking Buddies simplifies the procedure of matching dog’s and their owners and finding live long playing buddies by connecting users effortlessly. Swipe to find your new best friends!

Unique Features

  • Users can easily login to the app and select their dogs’ preferences. A profile with the main information and pictures is created quickly. Filters can be used to identify the perfect match.
  • Users have two options to match with other users. Either swiping intuitivly left or right, or by clicking on the intended icons.
  • Having a match, there is only one more step before meeting in real life! Chatting with users to exchange more information and agreeing on a location to meet is easy with the integrated messaging feature.

User Persona

Users are dog owners who take care of their dog’s social life. From puppy stage to grown up, from badly trained or afraid to open minded and friendly dogs.

Barking Buddies’ color palette embodies a minimalistic, clean and modern design. The primary colors are green and blue and white, supported by black, yellow and natural tones seen on dog illustration.

Typography

For Barking Buddies I chose Roboto as the font for the android version and SF Pro Display as the font for the iOS version.
